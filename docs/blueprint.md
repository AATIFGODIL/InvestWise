# **App Name**: InvestWise

## Core Features:

- Streamlined Onboarding: Simple sign-up process with an intuitive design to guide first-time investors, reducing initial overwhelm.
- Curated Investment Bundles: Pre-built investment bundles (e.g., Tech Starter Pack) allowing beginners to invest without deep research, offering a diversified starting point.
- Risk Assessment Tool: Short risk tolerance quiz to provide tailored investment suggestions, aligning with individual financial comfort levels.
- Visual Portfolio Tracking: Visual progress tracking with simple charts and graphs to show portfolio growth and progress towards set financial goals, enhancing user motivation.
- Mobile Investment Management: Mobile-friendly interface for easy buying and selling of investments, allowing users to manage their portfolio on the go.
- AI-Powered Investment Chatbot: A chatbot tool that answers common investment questions and explains financial terms.
- Community Investment Trends: Anonymous insights into what others in the same age group are investing in, providing inspiration and community insights without compromising privacy.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft, muted green (#A7D1AB) to convey stability and growth without feeling intimidating.
- Background color: Very light, desaturated green (#F0FAF2) to create a clean and calming environment.
- Accent color: A light desaturated yellow-green (#C4D793) to highlight important CTAs and information.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, strikes a balance of approachability and professionalism, appropriate for both headings and body text.
- Use simple, clean icons that are intuitive and modern, aiding navigation and feature recognition without overwhelming the user.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience and provide feedback, such as loading animations or confirmations upon actions.
